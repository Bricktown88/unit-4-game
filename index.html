Crystal Collector!

  • You will be shown a random number at the beginning of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. Each crystal has a random value.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  • If your score goes OVER the random number, you lose!
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each new game will assign new values to the crystals.
